NEW EMPLOYEE ONBOARDING GUIDE
==============================

Welcome to Acme Robotics! This guide walks you through your first two weeks.

DAY 1: ACCOUNT SETUP
---------------------
- IT will email you your company account credentials before your start date.
- Log into the company portal at portal.acmerobotics.internal and set up
  multi-factor authentication (MFA) using the Okta Verify app.
- Join the #new-hires channel on Slack and introduce yourself.
- Your manager will schedule a welcome call for your first morning.

DAY 1-3: REQUIRED TRAINING
---------------------------
All new employees must complete the following training modules within the
first 3 days, accessible through the Learning Management System (LMS):
  1. Information Security Basics (45 minutes)
  2. Code of Conduct and Anti-Harassment Training (60 minutes)
  3. Data Privacy Fundamentals (30 minutes)

Completion is tracked automatically and reported to HR. Employees who do not
complete training within 3 days will receive a reminder from HR on day 4.

WEEK 1: TEAM INTEGRATION
-------------------------
Your manager will assign an onboarding buddy from your team. The buddy's job
is to answer day-to-day questions, walk you through team-specific tools, and
have lunch with you at least once during your first week.

By the end of week 1, you should have:
  - Met one-on-one with your manager to review your 30/60/90-day goals
  - Set up your local development environment (engineering roles) or your
    core SaaS tool access (non-engineering roles)
  - Reviewed the team wiki and recent project documentation

WEEK 2: FIRST PROJECT TASK
----------------------------
Your manager will assign a small, well-scoped starter task by the start of
week 2. This task is intentionally low-risk and is meant to help you learn
the codebase or workflows, not to test your skills. You are encouraged to
ask questions liberally during this period; the team would much rather you
ask than guess.

BENEFITS ENROLLMENT
---------------------
You have 30 days from your start date to enroll in health, dental, and
vision insurance through the benefits portal. If you do not enroll within
this window, you will need to wait until the next open enrollment period
(typically in November) unless you experience a qualifying life event.

The company matches 401(k) contributions up to 4% of your salary, and you
become eligible to enroll in the 401(k) plan on the first day of the month
following your start date.

QUESTIONS?
-----------
Reach out to your onboarding buddy, your manager, or the HR team at
hr@acmerobotics.com for anything not covered in this guide.
